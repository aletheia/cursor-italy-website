We took over **Cafezal Brunch & Specialty Coffee Hub** in Milano for the day and invited Cursor users to swing by.

**📅 Date:** February 26, 2026  
**🕡 Time:** 10:00 – 18:30 CET  
**📍 Location:** [Cafezal Brunch & Specialty Coffee Hub](https://maps.app.goo.gl/cafezal-milano), Viale Premuda 14, 20129 Milano

Bring your laptop and spend some time building with other Cursor users. We had Cursor credits and coffee waiting, and you could also just pop by for coffee and to say hi.

Engineers from the Cursor team and Cursor power users were around throughout the day at the café—perfect for asking questions and sharing what you're building.

### Co-working slots

- **10:00 – 13:00** — Morning co-working
- **14:00 – 18:00** — Afternoon co-working
- **10:00 – 18:30** — Drop in anytime for coffee and a chat

### What to expect

- Co-working alongside other Cursor users
- Cursor credits and coffee
- Cursor team and power users on hand
- Casual networking and demos

Thanks to everyone who joined—186 of you came. We had a photographer taking pictures and videos for the event; these may be shared with the brand and hosts and on socials.
